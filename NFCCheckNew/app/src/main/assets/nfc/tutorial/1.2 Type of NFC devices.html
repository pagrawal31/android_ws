Type of NFC devices

Passive NFC

Passive NFC Devices include tags, and other small transmitters, that can send information to other NFC devices without the need for a power source of their own. However, they don’t really process any information sent from other sources, and can’t connect to other passive components. These often take the form of interactive signs on walls or advertisements.

Mostly Passive NFC devices will be without batteries, thus the cost of such devices will be very low.

You might have seen following passive NFC devices:
  • Metro Coin
  • Metro Card
  • Credit Card with chip
  • On Shopping items like clothes, shoes etc.

Active NFC

Devices which can not only transmit, but also receive the signal and take action upon the received signal. Active NFC will have battery support with them as they need to process the signal which requires power.

You might have seen following passive NFC devices:
  • Car/item Tracker
  • NFC in mobile which can communicate with other NFC enabled phone.
